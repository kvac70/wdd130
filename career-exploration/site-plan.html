Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide students with information for a successful career search. Users will be linked to other helpful resources for the purpose of expanding their knowledge and awareness of different career paths. This is intended to give the user hope and excitement for their future.

Audience

The audience for this website is students that need help guaging their interests and finding a path that will fulfill their desires to pursue a career in a field that allows them to engage in practicing what they're interested in.

Content

Home page

Are you looking for a career that you will really love? Tired of the old one, or even finding one for the first time? Well you have come to the right place! We've got exactly what you need to explore your options. Our website will contain a list of possible degrees, providing you with information to help you find a career path. There will be resources available through linked tests, articles, and real world advice. These options will help you find a path that best suits you and your interests. We'd love to help you achieve your goals and find joy in your career. People will come to the site if they are not sure about their future and if they desire to broaden their horizons. It will also appeal to people that aren't comfortable with where they are currently at in their career paths. Visitors will expect the site to help them understand in what career they may find the most success and happiness based on their interests and abilities. They will also expect to receive some sort of direction in which they can aim to find such a career path. Options for activities to engage in on the site will include linked tests to evaluate interests, educational articles to provide important information on career options, and items of advice from employed individuals in a particular field. It will be necessary to have access to a career search test, educational articles for reading, and items of advice from people who have successfully found a career in which they've found joy. People will want to visit this site because of the personalized actions they can take to find a path leading them to a job that will suit them.

Your Career Options

There are many options for career paths. The difficult part is finding the one that suits you and your interests. No one wants to consigned to what I refer to as endless woe. Endless woe is the state of a man or woman's soul within their occupational life where they begin to lose interest in their profession and, as a result, their job becomes rather bland. No one wants a bland job. More often than not, this lack of excitement will ooze into other parts of their life too (family life, recreation, times of leisure). So ... Let's explore out options! Because there are so many options, however, many paths will not be represented here, but what we've provided gives a good spread for our purpose.

Finding Your Career

Now that you have found something that interests you, we want to start to narrow things down. Again, when it comes to this sort of thing we will not be able to identify all the possible career options for you but we sure can call upon other resources for some help in our efforts. Here we provide you with suggestions to take career compatibility tests, read educational articles that are informative in terms of what is expected of a person working in the workforce. In addition, we've done our best to provide real world advice from folks that may have navigated similar challenges as the ones that brought you here. Please, roam around a while. We hope you find something that will present you with great future possibilities. What are options for your next steps. So now what? We've absorbed all this information and now it's time to apply, but how do we move forward? It is important to weigh out your possibilities that we've discussed before. After doing that, you should do some research to look into local job opportunities within your field of choice. If you are lacking the educational prerequisites, then it will be necessary for you to find a means by which you can receive the proper training. There may be many ways in which this can be done. Maybe in a university class setting or an infield training opportunity. We can't give you all the answers but you must know that it's game time now and the search is on.
